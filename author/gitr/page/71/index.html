WordPress 2.1 – Ella

January 23, 2007 By Gitr 6 Comments

I logged in this morning to a new suprise: Ella. It looks like Matt and crew have been busy, with 2 updates in about a week. I seems like yesterday that I installed 2.0.7. Here’s the install notes from WordPress.org.

EDIT #1: I have noticed that my blogroll is gone. They changed the command to pull links, which isn’t the problem. The problem is that they also tossed the table control we had over the list so that I have no choice but to display the category name and recreate all my heading styles because I don’t want gray, underlined category headings. Until they release a fix, my blogroll is down, because I spent days getting it the way I wanted it the first time.

EDIT #2: I bit the bullet and spent 15 minutes with my stylesheet to get the color and overlapping fixed. The blogroll is back up. I’ll figure out a way to get them in the order I want, but I refuse to use numbers…for now.

A Third of the Way Through 55

January 22, 2007 By Gitr 3 Comments

I ran all around kingdom come last night and turned in some quests and picked up some quests along the way. I went to Undercity to fly to Hammerfall to hit 300 First Aid, as Rhone handed me 2 more stacks of Runecloth to help. I hit 300 and flew to Tarren Mill to ride to Light’s Hope Chapel. That was a totally eventless ride.

Then I hearthed to Orgrimmar to fly to Winterspring and ride through Timbermaw Hold to get to Moonglade. I had 2 turn-ins there, and then it was off to Un’Goro to get to Silithus. I arrived in Silithus and picked up 2 kill quests and logged off for the night. After someone in guild chat spoiled the ending of the Patriots Colts game that I was taping on the DVR to watch the second half this morning. Monkey.

I got on this morning and ran out of the inn at Cenarian Hold and ran off to kill some Dredge Strikers and other varmin that I remember have incredibly poor drop rates. After 30 minutes, I had my 15 Strikers and 2 drops that I needed. I turned in the Striker quest and picked up the Dredge Crusher quest. I ran back out and killed 2 Crushers before I decided that it is better to make money to pay the bills than play a game, and left early for work. I am right on the cusp of 30% and have 100k rest XP. After work today and during the night tonight, that should be enough rest to get me through this level with double XP all the way.

I’m feeling pretty stinking powerful right now, and I think that is a major part in wanting to level Deadr to take him to Outlands over playing Gitr. Gitr doesn’t feel powerful. Sure he’s got the armor and HP to take a beating, but I can do things with Deadr that Gitr drools over, and I’ve only got 10 1/2 days of playtime and 55 levels so far. Deadr has 4500 MP, unbuffed, and can take down level 60s with 40-60% mana left. It’s a real trip to lay waste to an area like that. I finally found a Devout Belt on the AH last night, so I bought it for 9g and got the Armor bonus.
