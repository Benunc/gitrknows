Ding 14; Huntr Is in Ghostlands

April 20, 2007 By Gitr 7 Comments

Huntr got to levels 12 and 13 last night and hit 14 this morning and logged right away to get to work. The process is going pretty smoothly for starting out with absolutely no money or equipment support. The biggest adjustments are:

  • no healing potions
  • no big bags (I’ve had 3 6-slotters drop)
  • not always enough to train (that is over now)

I got Butterbanker to SM and bought a couple of things for 2s in hopes that they go for 10-12s like they should. Huntr is doing okay with money now, so I will start sending Butterbanker money after each training session.

Leveling with a hunter is certainly easier than it was with a mage, as there is positively no downtime right now. Unless I do something stupid, I also have no fear of dying, but that’s not to say I haven’t. I hate murlocs, by the way.

Feed-Stealers Beware

April 17, 2007 By Gitr 2 Comments

I have found a method of solving those pesky feed-stealers.

  1. You must have a current [tag]Creative Commons License[/tag] posted that covers any infringement.
  2. You must be able to track their IP address from your trackbacks, pingbacks, or spam folder.
  3. You must provide a link to your actual posts.
  4. You must contact the DNS provider/host for the domain. I used [tag]Network-Tools.com[/tag].
  5. You must be able to support that they are infringing upon your [tag]copyright[/tag].

Today I was able to do that against someone who has been stealing my feed for weeks. It only took an hour or so to get the whole thing done, and now I’m seeing a satisfying message on their domain:

This Account Has Been Suspended

Please contact the billing/support department as soon as possible.
