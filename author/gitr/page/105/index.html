AH craziness

September 12, 2006 By Gitr Leave a Comment

The AH on Kargath is way out of control right now, with it being such a mature server. Everyone has high-level toons now, or guildies that buy them what they need, so there’s no need to keep the prices of some of the essential level 60 mats or consumables.

Sunday, Major Healing pots were 13G for a 5 stack. Heart of Fires are not to be found. Herbs for raiding potions, forget it! The only way to get ahead in the market now is to get drops and sell them. It is not cost-effective to make healing, mongoose, swiftthistle, or rage pots at this point because the mats equal the selling price. Now, I made a nice penny off the pots that I got from Un’Goro and the soil that I harvested, but that’s small potatoes to what was going on before the summer.

Major playing time

September 12, 2006 By Gitr Leave a Comment

Gitr got in some long hours last night. After some very unsatisfying AH work, it was off to Un’Goro to pick up the flight path to Marshall’s Point. After a while of slaughtering tar monsters and picking up some major healing potions, it was back to IF to sell off my loot. I brought up my cooking to 225 and attempted to get the mats necessary to go back to Tanaris to get to 300, but the AH was wiped clean. So I sent Grimr 50G and all the fish I made and logged Gitr.

Next up was Grimr. I took him to get stocked up on arrows, food, and sweet nectar and headed to Stranglethorn. I met Stitches along the way and had completely forgotten about Feign Death. Three seconds before I died, a lvl 60 mage showed up and did a real number on him. On to Stranglethorn again.

I went to my normal upper 30’s hunting ground (he was about 40% of the way through 37) to the 37s and 38s gorillas. I was getting 460 and 480 rested xp for those and did that until I stumbled on King Bag and made my way to the shadowmaw panthers that I still had the quest for. I killed 10 of them and finished off the tigers in the area to make my way to the expedition camp to turn in the quest. I came up 400xp short and had to kill some 31s to level. Next in the quest line is the Panther boss.

I hearthed back to SW and started buying up leather and bargain-basement prices until they ran out and started working on my leatherworking. 50G goes a long way in the 100s. I think I only spent 13g on skills, but got the products up to the level 20 range.

Playing again

September 11, 2006 By Gitr Leave a Comment

Gitr’s creator is getting married, got promoted at work, and was having trouble with the WoW installation. Our registries are filling up with goodies, and my fiance has 2 showers this month (I hope for everyone’s sake she takes more showers this month than that).

Despite all of those things, Gitr playing again. We’re trying to figure out how much ‘alone time’ each of us will need when we’re co-habitating. Sunday, she came over and took a nap while I both listened to a sucky football game (Bucs and Ravens) and played WoW at the same time. That did me all right. I killed two birds with one stone.
