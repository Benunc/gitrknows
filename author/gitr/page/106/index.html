I’m back, again

July 24, 2006 By Gitr 3 Comments

I took a month off, and I’ve decided that WoW still has the most interest and gameplay of any of the games I have. I even dabbled in Maple Story, Entropia, and other free MMORPGs. They are either uninteresting, difficult to figure out the controls and UI, or not really free.

I created a new pally one night and got him up to level 7, but started playing Gitr again on Saturday. I’ve got him back to place where he can earn enough money to have fun. All of my toons were dead broke when I stopped a month ago. Gitr had 44s to his name.

I took him to Burning Steppes and started killing the little packs of fire demons in the lava rivers and made my way to the orcs west of the Ruins to get their money and drops. Very soon I had enough money to get a few Heart of Fires and started up with the transmutations. Then I started back up with AH scanning for deals and reselling. It really doesn’t take much when you know what you’re doing.

This morning I logged in with Grimr and transmuted an Arcanite Bar that I sent him the crystal for last night. 4 more bars to go for my Lionheart Helm.

The next thing on my list was continuing my Heroism questline. I looked all over my log for what quest I was working on. Then I went to the king’s room in IF to see where she wanted me to go. She had nothing of importance to say. After looking at the entire process on Wowwiki, I remembered that I still have to do a 45 minute Baron run. SHOOT!

I’ll get right on that.

The sky is falling

June 2, 2006 By Gitr 2 Comments

On June 14th my subscription to WoW ends. “But it’s only $15/month!” Right. I know it’s not much, it’s barely more than taking my girlfriend to a nicer medium-fast restaurant like Quiznos or Boston Market. Once. It’s that I also want to shave $15 off my phone bill by switching wireless service, $10 by getting rid of VOIP, and $15 by not using the air conditioner as much.

This week, my girlfriend came over and started sprucing the place up, married style to see what it looks like, and I must admit, it looks great…even if I do have a pink washcloth in MY bathroom. Granted, everything else in there is stainless steel or gray, but PINK!! Nice touch, actually. Now there are pillows, containers on the counter, a rug in the laundry room, and all kinds of nice touches. I need to marry her NOW. It won’t be now, but I don’t think we’ll wait until the time of year that we were planning on before the big move-out.

So, when I finish up with Call of Duty 2 that her parents bought me for Christmas, I’ll be back to play the expansion. If the Burning Legion isn’t out yet, I’ll go back to Rainbow Six or other Clancy “covert invisible spy” game that I can’t remember the name. It might even be fun to load up the good old Warcraft 2 or 3 for some custom scenarios.
