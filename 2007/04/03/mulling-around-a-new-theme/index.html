Mulling Around a New Theme

April 3, 2007 By Gitr 9 Comments

Lance and Tomas, along with others, have noted that comments are getting downright buggy now. I think there must be something in the code for my comments.php, so instead of trouble-shooting that and taking things offline and risk losing everything (AGAIN!), I went theme-hunting. 

I hunted high and low for something that will allow me to update my toons’ portraits in a prominent place and also attempt to shorten the page by adding one more sidebar. It is going to take some monkeying around with the margins and padding, but I think I can muster a good layout from this:

I have already edited it for most of the colors I want, but I still have several overhauls to do that I have code already existing in my posts for:

  • smilies have boxes
  • images don’t have padding
  • some links are bold, they will all need to be normal
  • I may need to stretch some of the columns out a tad

This is your first preview. What do you think? Lemme have it if it sucks. Give me props until the sun goes down if you like it.

EDIT: Here is an updated version. I will look into moving the navigation, but the links column needs to be widest because of the long names. Maybe I’ll move the widest sidebar to the far right.
