Deadr’s Journey Into Outlands at 58

January 30, 2007 By Gitr 9 Comments

I have long been saying that I’d be taking Deadr into Hellfire Peninsula as soon as he hit 58. I wasn’t kidding. I was on a bat within 5 minutes of dinging. I only took the time to repair and train up before getting Deadr on a bat.

In this post, I am detailing my journey through the Outlands as of yet. I logged off at 16% of the way through level 58, and it came really, really fast (40 minutes, including getting there). I couldn’t believe how much easier HP is for Deadr than Mr. Level 60 Gitr. 🙂

On the staircase just inside the Portal, I picked up Arrival in Outland, which sent me to the right to the Horde FlightMaster, Viagga Freyfeather. She gave me the quest, Journey to Thrallmar, which only takes hopping on a windrider and going to Thrallmar, the Horde base camp.

Upon arrival in Thralmar, you greet a soldier by the flight point (I’ll insert the name later) and he sends you outside to speak with Sergeant Shatterskull, who is just East of the gate, down by some supplies.

Before you go outside the walls, go see Megzeg Nukklebust who will give you the quest, I Work…For the Horde! This one is really easy, and just requires you running around as usual and picking up 8 Salvaged Metal and 8 Salvaged Wood, very reminiscent of the broken race car parts quest from Shimmering Flats. I also picked up Eradicate the Burning Legion and Bonechewer Blood, which I will detail when I get to those tonight.

Sergeant Shatterskull gives you Felspark Ravine, which parallels the Alliance quest of killing demons in The Path of Anguish. It also requests 6 Infernal Warbringers, 4 Flamewalker Imps, and 1 Dreadcaller to die. This was far, far, easier than with Gitr.

 

I’d been fighting things harder than these in Eastern Plaguelands the night before. The Imps die on their own after putting Shadow Word: Pain on them. 🙂 I returned to Sergeant Shatterskull with my kills and he gave me The Staff of Twin Worlds and sent me away to speak with Forward Commander To’arch for Forward Base: Reaver’s Fall. I’ll do that when I get home.

As I turned in my quest, a very strange thing happened. This dude shouted this, and the NPCs were all under attack from Infernals, I was stunned, but not hurt, and it was all over in 30 seconds.

No trip to Outlands with 300 skill in any trade would be complete without getting some skillz, so I saw Dalinna, the Tailor, and got me an upgrade on my abilities.

 

My spell damage increased to 91 for all spells, I have over 2000 armor with Inner Fire, 3100 HP, and almost 5200 MP. It’s time to rock!
