Vacation Day #3: Remembering Visitor 1,000

February 19, 2007 By Gitr Leave a Comment

There once was a day when “finally” hitting 1,000 visitors was a big deal. Looking back on it now, I find this post funny to myself, although I can see my true delight at this milestone back in December 2005. My co-workers and I put together some predictions about traffic for my blog this year, and it is already wildly wrong. I went pretty optimistic with a smooth linear growth line for the year. Much to all our amazement, on the 13th, I passed the traffic stats for the entire month of January and my average daily visits is quadruple what it was on the first of the year.

Thank you all for reading and being a part of this. I know I’m not in the same league as WoWInsider, but the number of visits I wake up to at 4:00 in the morning makes me /grin at this old post:

We have a winner!! Winner of what, I have no idea.

At 10:43:56 am Intoxication was the 1,000th visitor, earning the coveted spot in Gitr’s WoW Blog history.
