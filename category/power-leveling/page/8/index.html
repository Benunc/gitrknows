Ding 44; Caliph Serpentsting Is Easy

June 8, 2007 By Gitr 4 Comments

3d 6h 3m /played time using Joana’s Horde Guide. I remember back in the day, Caliph Serpentsting in Tanaris was nearly impossible to find and kill. I have now killed him 4 times because he give triple Gadgetzan reputation in addition to being the highest level mob in the area. I got everything in Tanaris done except for the Shipping Schedule refused to drop and the 6 footlockers that I did get did not contain the Middle Map Fragment. I’m looking for that on the AH.

I’m off to Feralas for what I know to be a ton of quick kill/drop quests, so I loaded up on arrows and flew to Freewind Post last night.

I discovered that I have placed some of my Beastmaster points in the wrong skill, so the next time I’m at a trainer, I’m going to drop my talents. I’m darn tempted to fly to Thunder Bluff this morning.

I found a great pair of gloves on the AH for 50g, and then realized that I can’t use them to the best of their ability unless I partake in BRK’s pet peeve of being a melee hunter. No thanks. I ‘d hate to become an object of one of his WoWinsider posts because of that.

I got over 50% of the way done with level 44 because I was taking on 3-5 pirates at a time and ripping them to shreds. I have reached a pretty good stride with making sure Tiggr hits each one of them before I heal him. Once the heal starts, usually one or two of them are down, and I can take that kind of beating for a time until he gets another one down and call him back for help. It’s a good system and I pity anyone without a 3k HP 4k Armor tank traveling with them. 🙂

Ding 41; Blew Through Level 40

May 30, 2007 By Gitr 2 Comments

I didn’t even have to grind one second for level 41 and got there in under 2 1/2 hours with a total /played time of 2d 19h 29m. I reached it before I was supposed to, most likely because of drop rates with the buzzard wings. Badlands is so good for leveling when there are quests associated with all of the killing. There are tons of mobs, but they aren’t really aggro nets like other places.

I think I’m done with Badlands, or at least for a while. I already killed the patrolling orge boss, a 45. I put Hunter’s Mark on him and sent in Tiggr to Intimidate him right off the bat. I struck him with Serpent Sting and kept nailing him with Arcane Shots as often as it cooled down. As soon as he went down, I started running because Tiggr was down to 15% health after taking a beating from 6 mobs at one time. I got away and came back 20 seconds later to loot the body.

I’ve got all the pages for the Green Hills of Stranglethorn, so I’m off to turn those in and run all the way to Swamp of Sorrows.

I see Ravenkiller hit level 30 last night. Gratz, man. What else did you all do last night?
