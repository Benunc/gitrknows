Ding 23; Deadr Hits 279 Enchanting

March 12, 2007 By Gitr 14 Comments

It’s been a very expensive week so far. I’ve earned over 2,300g and spent 2,280 of it on more mats and leveling my enchanting. I am at the point now that I’m DEing armor for Dream Dust for 10 dust per shield enchant and selling all of the Greater Nether Essences that come off them and the here and there weapon purchase. They sell for nearly 14g per pair when they drop 2 at a time off a weapon. I sold about 15 bags over the weekend, but this morning, cloth prices were obscene, so I skipped that.


Astranon finally joins the guild.
Bloodr had some help from Sunforge last night in Thousand Needles to kill Snapjaw. I got the quest in Thunder Bluff when I was turning in a “go over yonder and speak to so-and-so quest” last week. We took him down and I logged for the night. I turned in the quest (a level 30, red one at that) and got to 94%xp. Shoooot. I flew back to Thousand Needles to kill some centaurs and found them to give me over 1% per kill. I wish it was always that easy. I dinged, took the elevator back up Freewind Post, re-stocked my arrows, got the centaur kill quest, and logged for work.

I think I’ll play her some more tonight, maybe get to 24.

Slowly Gaining Aldor Reputation with Marks of Kil’jaeden

March 8, 2007 By Gitr 5 Comments

I ventured out into the Bone Wastes this morning with 3 things on my mind: Marks of Kiljaeden, Netherweave Cloth, and killing 20 Bonelashers.

I needed to get 10 Marks of Kil’jaeden over to Adyen the Lightwarden in Shattrath City for finishing the first quest in that repeatable faction rep questline. I read somewhere that I need to turn in 22 stacks of 10 for 5500 rep with Aldor. This will take a while.

I ended up with 10 Marks of Kil’jaeden, 10 Netherweave Cloth, 7 Bonelashers, and the Cabal Report to turn in at the caravan.

I was getting pretty good drop rate from just about every Cabal Skirmisher, but I don’t recall any Cabal Anjurist dropping them. I only saw 2 Cabal Tomb-Raider dudes, so it’s a bit too early for me to tell if they do…without checking Thottbot, which I won’t. 🙂

Deadr Is a Mooncloth Tailor – A How-To Guide

March 8, 2007 By Gitr Leave a Comment

I visited Nasmara Moonsong in Lower City of Shattrath last night to pick up a much cheaper copy of Primal Mooncloth than what was listed in the AH. To my delight, it was only a 4g recipe. I saw that she was also a questgiver, and one for a tailoring specialty, to boot. I checked the other two specializations of Tailoring and picked Mooncloth for one reason, I want to be able to use the bags that I craft. The Shadowcloth 20-slot bag is for Warlocks only. I am obviously drawn to the power of those crafted pieces with +Shadow dmg, but what the heck good is a bag that I can’t use. Besides, I can always use the +Healing gear in instances that take just a bit more umph for healing while remaining Shadow spec for VE healing and soloing.

Back to Moonsong. She’s located at 66,69 in Shattrath City and gives the quest Becoming a Mooncloth Tailor. She gives you a Square of Imbued Netherweave, which you need to take to the moonwell in Cenarian Refuge in Zangarmarsh. That done, I received 11,000xp and became a Mooncloth Tailor. Venturing Out In Terrokar Forest; Tailoring=$$$

March 2, 2007 By Gitr 18 Comments

I got on Bloodr again last night to find the two places I wanted to go to turn in quests and do some more of them were still being camped by high-level Alliance players. /sigh. So Deadr went and made a ton of gold. In the process, he spent a lot, too.

I started yesterday morning getting the quest for getting the two Eyes of Veil, but hadn’t ventured out to do so. I got to the location after work and got one of them, but I still need to get the Eye of Shienor in the treehouse on the East side. During that stint, I lit the 3 Altars and got 14/30 of the feathers from the guys guarding the eyes and about a stack of Netherweave cloth (yay for saving 5g!).

I went to UC to play the AH to get cloth and such to make more bags. After 9 bags sold right after posting them, I repeated the process, but made 5 Netherweave Boots so I could get some more skill points and got to 245.

This morning, all of the bags had sold and I went in to buy more. One of the sellers /w me and said “buy them ALL!” LOL. He was saving for an epic flying mount.
